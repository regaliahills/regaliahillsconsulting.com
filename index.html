Before You Hire Us

Discovery

Weeks 1–2

We don't ask you to commit before we've done the work to understand your business. Every engagement starts with a focused discovery process — at no cost to you — so that both sides can make a fully informed decision.

Intro Call — A call with the owner so we understand how the business operates, the long-term goals, and where the business stands financially.
Financial Review — We examine your financials to identify gaps, risks, and the highest-leverage opportunities.
Accounting & Systems Review — A look at your current processes, software, and operational infrastructure.
Discovery Proposal Delivered — A clear written document outlining exactly what we found and what we'd do about it.

Phase One

Build the Foundation

Months 1–4

Every business we work with is different. Some have strong accounting in place and need the financial operating system built on top of it. Others need foundational work first. Phase One is about assessing where things stand and ensuring the right infrastructure is in place — so everything that follows is built on solid ground.

Stabilize & Clarify — Map how revenue is generated, where margin is being made or lost, and how the business operates at a financial level.
Accounting Foundation — Ensure your accounting processes, chart of accounts, and close process are accurate, consistent, and built to support real decision-making. Where gaps exist, we address them.
Install the Financial Operating System — Build cash flow forecasting, job-level profitability reporting, KPI dashboards, and the reporting cadence your business needs to make real decisions.
Set the Roadmap — Define clear ownership, accountability, and a forward-looking financial model aligned to your actual business goals.
Phase Two

Operate & Grow

Month 4+ Ongoing

With the foundation in place, we shift into ongoing financial leadership. This is where our focus areas become embedded in how your business operates — not a report you receive, but a rhythm you run by.

Monthly Financial Review — Full P&L, balance sheet, and cash flow with plain-language commentary on what drove the numbers.
Recurring CFO Calls — Scheduled touchpoints to review performance, flag risks, and support your biggest decisions.
Forward-Looking Forecasts — Live financial models, rolling cash flow projections, and annual budgets that update as the business evolves.
Strategic Advisory — Scenario modeling, pricing support, growth planning, and the decision-making infrastructure to scale with confidence.

About the Firm

Built on relationships.
Driven by results.

Regalia Hills Consulting was founded on a simple belief: small business owners deserve the same caliber of financial leadership that large companies take for granted — delivered by people who genuinely care about their success.

As brothers, we built this firm around the values we were raised with: relationships first, a deep commitment to serving others, and the conviction that real partnership means showing up not just as advisors, but as invested members of your team. We love what we do because it puts us in the room with driven, passionate business owners — and gives us the opportunity to bring clarity, confidence, and strategic direction to the financial side of everything they've built.

Our mission is straightforward: help small, owner-led businesses gain the financial clarity they need to make better decisions, grow sustainably, and build something that lasts.

John
Co-Founder & Partner

John started his career in audit and has spent the last six years working directly alongside small business owners to bring financial clarity to their operations. He specializes in cleaning up the books, implementing modern accounting systems and technology, securing financing, building cash flow projections and scenario models, and standing up HR and operational infrastructure. John handles the complexities of the back office so founders can stay focused on their vision — serving as a true strategic partner who ensures every financial decision and operational system supports where the business is going.

Andrew
Co-Founder & Partner

Andrew brings a decade of experience across audit, consulting, and corporate finance — working with clients and business owners to help them accomplish their goals. He has a deep curiosity for understanding how companies actually run and make a profit, and a talent for turning complex financial information into a clear story that owners can act on. Andrew believes that great financial leadership isn't about the numbers themselves — it's about what those numbers tell you, and what you do next.
